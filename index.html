Meet the Owner

Alexander Silveira

Owner & CEO of A.S. Home Services

Alexander Silveira is the founder and owner of A.S. Home Services, a locally operated home services company based in the Tampa Bay area. Built on hard work, reliability, and attention to detail, Alexander started the company with the goal of helping homeowners maintain clean, professional-looking properties while delivering honest service and strong communication.

With experience in hands-on labor, exterior cleaning, customer service, and project coordination, Alexander focuses on building a company that homeowners can trust for dependable and consistent results. His passion for entrepreneurship, professionalism, and helping people has driven the growth of A.S. Home Services into a company focused on quality work and long-term customer relationships.

Alexander believes that every home deserves proper care and that small details make a big difference in curb appeal and property value. Through A.S. Home Services, his mission is to provide Tampa Bay homeowners with reliable exterior services that make maintaining a home easier and stress-free.

Built with hard work, consistency, and pride in every job.
